At My Best

This version follows the booklet idea that these are really two sets of cards in one: strength words and photographs. Use the words to notice what you do best, then use the photo side to open up richer reflection and team conversation.

How the booklet frames the deck

"What are the At My Best Strengths Cards? Cards. 48 of them. Strengths. Photos. Simple."

"Do the photographs relate to the words? Yes, but only loosely, so don't worry if you can't see the connection. For some exercises you might use the strengths, for others you'll use the photographs. Think of them as two sets of cards in one."
"How do I use them? To prompt thinking and start conversations around what people do best. Beyond that, it's up to you, we'd encourage you to experiment and see what works."
