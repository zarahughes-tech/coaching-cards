Coaching Cards for Teams

50 tried and tested coaching questions to stimulate conversation, improve team relationships and create more effective team working. Question cards for the group; process cards for the facilitator.

How to use this deck

The deck includes both question cards and process cards. Question cards are prompts for the team to explore together. Process cards are facilitation structures you can use privately or bring into the room intentionally.

About this deck

For use by managers, team leaders, and coaches in team coaching sessions, group work, and meetings.

Written by Kim Morgan, owner and director of Barefoot Coaching Ltd. 42 question cards and 8 process cards.
