Coaching Cards for Teams

This version separates facilitator process cards from team-facing question cards, so you can lead a session without mixing prompts and instructions together.

How to use this deck

The photographed set includes both question cards and process cards. Question cards are prompts for the team. Process cards are facilitation structures you can use privately or bring in intentionally.

Box copy

50 tried and tested coaching questions to stimulate conversation, improve team relationships and create more effective team working.

For use by managers, team leaders and coaches in team coaching sessions, group work and meetings.

Written by Kim Morgan, owner and director of Barefoot Coaching Ltd.
