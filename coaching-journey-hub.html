Foundation

Know Yourself Deeply

Everything begins with self-knowledge. These three tools help you understand who you are at your best, what strengths define your work, and what values guide your decisions. This foundation is where clarity, confidence, and intentionality are born.

✨
At My Best
96 moments of excellence. Discover the recurring patterns that define you when you are at your finest.
96 cards
💪
Strong Suits
Professional strengths across 4 dimensions. Understand what you do best and the shadows that come with it.
40 cards
🧭
The Values Cards
Clarify what you stand for and what guides your most important decisions through sorting and reflection.
56 cards
Phase 1 Exercise: Create Your Personal Profile
After completing all three foundation tools, write a 1-page personal profile that combines them: "At my best, I am [At My Best insight], my professional strengths are [Strong Suits], and I am guided by [Values]." This becomes your compass for the rest of the journey.
2

Practice

Show Up Intentionally

Knowledge without practice remains potential, not power. These two tools give you daily ways to embody your strengths and values, both individually and with your team. Small, consistent practices compound into lasting change.

☀️
Coaching Cards for Every Day
50 daily practices. Morning rituals, mindful pauses, and evening reflections that build your strength day by day.
50 cards
🤝
Coaching Cards for Teams
42 reflection questions and 8 team processes. Build psychological safety, trust, and shared clarity with your team.
50 cards
Phase 2 Exercise: 30-Day Daily Practice
Commit to drawing one card from Coaching Cards for Every Day each morning for 30 days. Spend 3 minutes with it, then journal one insight. Over the month, you will notice shifts in how you show up: more intentional, more aligned with your strengths and values.
3

Transformation

Break Through What Is Stopping You

Growth eventually hits a ceiling: a pattern you cannot break, a change you cannot sustain, a belief that limits you. These two tools help you understand what is really blocking progress and move through it with clarity and courage.

🔓
Intelligent Change Closer
109 questions across 3 nested levels. Navigate personal resistance, uncover competing commitments, and find the pathway forward.
109 cards
🛡️
Immunity to Change
A 5-stage diagnostic framework. Map your hidden assumptions and understand exactly why you are immune to change, then design an experiment to test them.
Framework
Phase 3 Exercise: Immunity Map
Choose one area where you feel stuck. Complete the Immunity to Change framework: What are you committed to? What competing commitment is in the way? What worry underlies it? What assumption makes that worry feel true? This clarity is your breakthrough.
4

Integration

Embody Your Growth

Transformation is not just cognitive. It is felt, embodied, intuited. This tool bridges your thinking mind with your emotional intelligence, helping you integrate insights into lived experience.

🎨
Picture Coaching Cards
51 evocative images. Use visual prompts to access your intuition, sense emerging patterns, and ground insights in your body.
51 cards
Phase 4 Exercise: Weekly Intuition Check-In
Every week, draw one card from Picture Coaching Cards. Sit with the image for 5 minutes without thinking. What arises? What is your gut telling you about your progress and what is next? This practice develops the wisdom that lives beyond your conscious mind.

Ready to Begin?

Start with the Foundation phase to understand yourself deeply. Or explore any tool that calls to you. There is no wrong order — follow your intuition and your needs.
