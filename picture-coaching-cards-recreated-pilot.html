How to use this deck

Pick the image that catches your attention first, not the one that feels easiest to explain. Use it as the opening into the conversation, then notice what feelings, memories, or metaphors come up.

Prompts

What is this image saying about where you are right now? What part of this photograph feels most like your current situation? If this image held advice for you, what would it be asking you to notice?

Now / next

Select a second card to compare where you are now with what feels possible next. What is shifting, what remains true, and what bridge sits between them?
