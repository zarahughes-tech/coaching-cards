Strong Suits

A simple reflection version of Strong Suits for self-coaching, 1:1 coaching, or team discussion. Pick a mode below, read each card’s strength statement first, then use the lower statement as the possible overuse or shadow side.

Start here

This version is not a multiplayer game. It works best as a solo reflection tool or as something a coach, facilitator, or team lead can use to prompt conversation. Choose one of the three ways in below.

Best starting point: choose Start with 5 cards if you want the closest thing to a full reflection exercise.

What the suits mean

♦ Standards

Reliable, objective, thorough.

♠ Drive

Determined, driven, assertive.

♥ Passion

Energetic, enthusiastic, persuasive.

♣ Relationships

Patient, agreeable, diplomatic.

How to read a card

Read the top statement as the strength you bring when you are using it well. Then read the lower statement as the possible overuse, blind spot, or shadow side of that same strength.
