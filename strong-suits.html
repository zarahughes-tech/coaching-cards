Exercise: Spot Your Patterns

Notice What You Have Selected

Are your strengths clustered in one suit or spread across all four? This tells you something important about how you work.

  • Heavy on Standards? You are the person who sets and holds the bar. People trust you for rigour, consistency, and care.
  • Heavy on Drive? You make things happen. Your strength is momentum: spotting what needs doing and doing it.
  • Heavy on Passion? You bring people with you. Your energy and creativity create the conditions for others to flourish.
  • Heavy on Relationships? You are the connector. Your strength is making people feel seen, heard, and part of something.
  • Balanced across all four? You are versatile. You can shift to what the moment requires.
