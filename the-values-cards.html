The Values Cards

This version follows the booklet method more closely: start with the whole deck visible together, sort into three piles, discard the lower two piles, then narrow what remains to 10, 5, 3, and finally 1.

How the booklet says to use them

“With the issue in mind, take your values cards in hand. Clear your mind of everything else as much as you can, and make your selections quickly, instinctively.”

Detailed Sorting Method

1. Sort the deck into really important, slightly important, and unimportant.

2. Discard the slightly important and unimportant piles.

3. Sort the really important pile again and set aside any values chosen only to solve a troubling issue.

4. Narrow what is left to your top 10, then 5, then 3 core values, then your number one value.
